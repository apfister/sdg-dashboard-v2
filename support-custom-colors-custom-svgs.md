### to add in support for custom fill colors on custom svgs

- open `node_modules/@esri/calcite-components/dist/esm-es5/calcite-icon.entry.js`

- find `, opacity:"opacity"` and add in the following right before it
  `fill:"fill" in t?t.fill:"currentColor",`

- in `myCustomIcon16.svg`, add a `fill: <colorHexCode>` key/value pair to a path object entry.
